About the app

Can I Kona? is a tool designed to help athletes track their chances of qualifying for both the IRONMAN World Championship in Kona, Hawaii and the IRONMAN 70.3 World Championship. It provides real-time age-graded results during races and serves as a repository of historical age-graded qualification data to help athletes understand their qualifying prospects for both championship events.
